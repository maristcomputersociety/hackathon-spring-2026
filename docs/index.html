What is the Marist Computer Society?

The Marist Computer Society is a student-run club at Marist University focused on Computer Science. Our mission is to cultivate a community for those studying or interested in Computer Science. Students seeking to build knowledge, test their skills, build connections, participate in workshops, discuss ideas with fellow students and professors, or showcase their skills will find ample opportunities within our club. We are open to all students, regardless of prior experience.

For years, we have been hosting hackathons on a semesterly basis, but this is the biggest post-covid hackathon we have hosted. We are excited to see what you all come up with!
